About Me

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Accounting at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

My research primarily examines the conflict of interests between local governments and members of the business community.

I am interested in the role of transparency in enabling accountability.

I will be on the job market in the coming academic year. Prior to joining the HKUST Ph.D. program, I attained a M.Sc. in Accoutning from the HKUST and a B.Sc. in Business Administration and Management from Bocconi University.
